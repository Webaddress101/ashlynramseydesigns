I've always been the one to sit back and observe my family members, which leads me to say I've always been adament on making my own path.

So, one day I was watching a YouTube video which involved a drawing of a celebrity, leading to me turning to my boyfriend and saying, "what about graphic design." I grabbed my computer and started searching up schools offering graphic design which is where I found FIRST. I originally graduated High School in Charleston, SC and two days later I decided to move to Savannah, GA. I lived there for almost a year than one day I was on the phone with my older sister and she brought up the idea of my boyfriend and I moving to Wisconsin with her and now I live in Wisconsin with my sister while attending class at FIRST. Going back to when I lived in Savannah my dad was a KJ where he would go to different locations and host karaoke nights. Growing up I was always around stuff like that and so my hobby became singing which has become a passion of mine and is also a huge part of my life. Usually when I meet people they wouldn't notice that I'm a huge introvert and I just love to stay home in my bed and binge on my shows and watch movies.
