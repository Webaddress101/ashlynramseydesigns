I've always been the one to sit back and observe my family members, which leads me to say I've always been adament on making my own path.

I graduated my High School in Charleston, SC and ended up moving to Savannah, Ga shortly after. For awhile, I just couldn't decide what I wanted to do with my life. Then, one day I was watching a YouTube video which involved a drawing of a celebrity, which got me thinking, "what about graphic design?" I grabbed my computer and started searching up schools offering graphic design which is where I found my college F.I.R.S.T. Institue. After a few months I decided to move to Wisconsin with my now fiance and family. Still attending F.I.R.S.T online. Growing up I was always around music and artsy things. Music has always been a passion of mine. Leading me to say that singing is my hobby and a bige part of my life. Meeting new people growing up I was anxious and scared, I still am at times. Ever sense I've become a student at F.I.R.S.T. that's brought me out of my shell. Although, I do love a good stay at home in my bed and binge on my shows and watch movies all day days.
